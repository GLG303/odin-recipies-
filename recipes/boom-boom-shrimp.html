Bang Bang Shrimp Tacos

Delicious Shrimp Tacos with a Kick!

These Bang Bang Shrimp tacos are amazingly delicious. Crispy, spicy awesomeness wrapped in a corn torilla. What more could you want!

Ingredients

Bang Bang Sauce

  • 1/3 Cup Mayo
  • 2 tsp Siracha
  • 3 tbsp Sweet chili sauce

Shrimp

  • 1 lb Shrimp peeled and deveined
  • 3/4 Cup Cornstarch
  • 1 tsp Kosher Salt

For Serving

  • Chopped Red Cabbage
  • Chopped Cilantro
  • Lime Wedges
  • 10 Corn Tortillas

Directions

Prepare the Bang Bang Sauce

  1. In a medium size mixing bowl combine mayo, siracha, and sweet chili sauce. Mix thourghly. Add a touch of sweet chili sauce for a sweeter taste or a touch more sirachi if you prefer more spice. Refridgerate for later.

Prepare the Shrimp

  1. Fill a large sauce pan with oil and heat over medium heat
  2. While oil is heating place cornstarch and salt in a bowl. Mix together.
  3. Coat shrimp with cornstarch and place in a seperate bowl
  4. After the shrimp is coated begin frying the shrimp in batches. Fry shrimp for 4-5 minuntes or until golden brown. Remove shrimp and place on a paper towel lined plate. Repeat for the second batch of shrimp.
  5. After shrimp is cooked place shrimp in a serving bowl and coat with the Bang Bang Sauce. Be sure to save some of the sauce for drizzeling on your tacos before serving!

Assemble the Tacos

  1. Fill tortillas with 4 to 5 shrimp
  2. Top with red cabbage
  3. Drizzle with extra Bang Bang Sauce
  4. Top with cilantro
  5. Enjoy!
