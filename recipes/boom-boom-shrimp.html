Bang Bang Shrimp Tacos

Delicious Shrimp Tacos with a Kick!

These Bang Bang Shrimp tacos are amazingly delicious. Crispy, spicy awesomeness wrapped in a corn torilla. What more could you want!

Ingredients

Bang Bang Sauce

  • 1/3 Cup Mayo
  • 2 tsp Siracha
  • 3 tbsp Sweet chili sauce

Shrimp

  • 1 lb Shrimp peeled and deveined
  • 3/4 Cup Cornstarch
  • 1 tsp Kosher Salt

For Serving

  • Chopped Red Cabbage
  • Chopped Cilantro
  • Lime Wedges
  • 10 Corn Tortillas

Directions

Prepare the Bang Bang Sauce

In a medium size mixing bowl combine mayo, siracha, and sweet chili sauce. Mix thourghly. Add a touch of sweet chili sauce for a sweeter taste or a touch more sirachi if you prefer more spice. Refridgerate for later.
